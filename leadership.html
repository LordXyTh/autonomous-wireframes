Secretary General

International Democracy Union

The Secretary General leads the IDU Secretariat, implementing the decisions of the Executive Committee and managing the day-to-day operations of the International Democracy Union. The Secretary General represents the IDU at international forums and coordinates cooperation between member parties.

With deep experience in international political cooperation and centre-right politics, the Secretary General works to strengthen democratic institutions worldwide and advance the IDU's mission of promoting freedom and democracy.
