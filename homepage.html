About the IDU

Defending Democracy Since 1983

The International Democracy Union (IDU) is a working group of centre-right political parties, which enables them to establish contacts and to co-operate on matters of policy and organisation.

Founded in London in 1983, the IDU now brings together over 80 member parties from across the globe, united by a commitment to freedom, democracy, the rule of law, and individual human rights.
